Data Protection Information

The privacy of all logged users is ensured by means of the European Data Protection Directive.

According to the Data Protection Directive, publication of information gathered from personal data not obtained from subjects directly should contain the information published on this page.

The website will only be hosted locally, on the machines of both the developers and the client.

The following types of information are stored in the tracking logs:

  • Username (Hashed before processing)
  • Device Mac Adress (Hashed before processing)
  • Date and time of log
  • Session duration of log, with increments of 5 minutes
  • Vicinity to Access Point
  • Signal strengt of Access Point
  • Location of Access Point

The username and mac address are hashed and therefore not directly linkable to a natural person and the type of machine he / she is using.

Every Eduroam user has the right to both access the data output and to rectify the data concerning him.
