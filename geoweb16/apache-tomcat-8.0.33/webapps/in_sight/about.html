About Project in_sight

Project in_sight is focused on the presence of people in sense of occupation and exploitation of available space.

and to extimate the number of devices at a location at a certain time, representing presence of people at that place at that time or for a certain duration.

The main client for this project is the TU Delft Facility Management & Real Estate (FMRE).

Their aim is to improve the conducting of resources and efforts by improving their understanding of the use of the TU Delft campus.

FMRE expects an estimation of the efficiency of the spaces covered in their real estate portfolio in order to be used for predictive occupation.


The project was started as a Synthesis Project for the MSc Geomatics for the Built Environment program.

The general scope of the project is the monitoring of flows and occupation patterns with wifi.


Research Question

To what extent can the alignment of occupation and exploitation of educational facilities

on different scales be indicated through WiFi monitoring?
